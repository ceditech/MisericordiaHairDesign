How to Maintain Your Knotless Braids for 8+ Weeks

Dede Williams

Master Braider • October 12, 2023 • schedule 8 min read

Knotless braids are more than just a style; they're an investment in your hair's health and your morning routine. With the right care, you can keep them looking fresh and frizz-free for two full months.

1 Daily Scalp Care

The foundation of long-lasting braids is a healthy scalp. Because your scalp is more exposed with braids, it's prone to dryness and buildup. I recommend using a lightweight, antimicrobial oil twice a week.

Dede's Pro Tip:

Avoid heavy greases which can cause "caking" at the roots. Look for peppermint or tea tree oils to soothe any itching without the heavy residue.

2 Nighttime Routine: Silk vs. Bonnet

Friction is the enemy of neat braids. When you toss and turn on cotton pillowcases, the fibers grab onto your hair, causing "flyaways" and frizz.

check_circle

Silk Scarves

Best for keeping the "lay" flat. Tie it snug around your edges to maintain that freshly-braided look.

check_circle

Satin Bonnets

Ideal for length protection. Ensure your bonnet is long enough (XL size) so your braids don't curl at the ends.

3 Washing Without the Frizz

Yes, you can (and should) wash your braids! The key is a focused approach. Dilute your shampoo in a spray bottle with water. Spray directly onto the scalp, massage gently, and let the suds run down the braids.

  • waves Always dry completely using a hooded dryer or cool blow dry settings. Damp braids lead to mildew.
  • dry_cleaning Use a microfiber towel to pat dry; never rub.
