# Design System: MHDESIGNS
**Project ID:** 387000833000817160

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The MHDESIGNS design system embodies an **Elegant, Radiant, and High-End** aesthetic. It is designed to evoke confidence and professionalism, focusing on the artistry of braiding while maintaining a clean, modern digital experience. The interface feels "Airy" and "Premium," utilizing generous whitespace and a sophisticated purple-leaning palette to create a sense of luxury and trustworthiness.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
The system uses a curated selection of purples and pinks to establish a regal and feminine brand identity.

*   **Sovereign Purple (#a319c5):** The primary brand color. Used for cornerstone actions, primary buttons, and critical branding elements.
*   **Deep Regal Plum (#8b15a8):** Secondary brand color. Primarily used for hover states, deep accents, and supporting brand marks.
*   **Radiant Orchid (#b84dd4):** Light brand variant. Used for lighter backgrounds, secondary highlights, and subtle borders.
*   **Soft Lavender (#d084e8):** Accent brand color. Used for decorative elements and soft transitions.
*   **Vibrant Violet (#7c3aed):** Auxiliary accent. Used sparingly for specific callouts or interactive states.
*   **Cloud Mist (#f8f6f7):** Primary light background. Provides a soft, clean canvas that allows the purple tones to pop.
*   **Midnight Plum (#221019):** Primary dark background. A deep, rich, and immersive base for dark mode.
*   **Pure Canvas (#ffffff):** Surface color for cards and elevated containers in light mode.

## 3. Typography Rules
*   **Font Family:** `Plus Jakarta Sans` is the sole typeface. It is a modern, geometric sans-serif that balances approachability with professional precision.
*   **Weight Usage:**
    *   **Bold/Extrabold:** Reserved for high-level headers (H1, H2) and primary navigation.
    *   **Medium/Semibold:** Used for subheaders (H3, H4), labels, and button text.
    *   **Normal:** Standard body text for maximum readability.
*   **Scale:** Follows a classic typographic scale from `xs` (0.75rem) to `9xl` (8rem) for dramatic hero sections.

## 4. Component Stylings
*   **Buttons:** Feature "Subtly rounded corners" (`rounded-lg` / 1rem) and utilize the **Sovereign Purple** for primary actions. They often include a "Brand Shadow" (`0 10px 25px -5px rgba(163, 25, 197, 0.3)`) to create depth.
*   **Cards/Containers:** Utilize "Generous rounding" (`rounded-xl` / 1.5rem) and "Whisper-soft diffused shadows" to stand out from the background. Backgrounds are typically **Pure Canvas** or a subtle **Cloud Mist**.
*   **Inputs/Forms:** Clean and minimalist with a "Subtle curve" (`0.375rem` / `sm`) and thin borders (`#e2e8f slate-200` equivalent).

## 5. Layout Principles
*   **Whitespace Strategy:** The system prioritizes "Generous breathing room." Large margins and significant vertical spacing (using custom 18 and 112 tokens) are used to separate major sections.
*   **Grid Alignment:** Follows a standard responsive grid system with a focus on centered hero layouts and multi-column service galleries.
*   **Transitions:** Uses "Smooth transitions" (`cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 1, 1)`) with a default duration of `200ms` for all interactive states, ensuring the UI feels responsive and alive.
