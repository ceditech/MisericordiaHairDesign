# Prompt for MHDESIGNS Multi-Step Booking Flow

Generate 4 distinct UI components (or one comprehensive page with 4 states) for the following booking steps, maintaining the "Elegant, Radiant, and High-End" aesthetic.

## Shared Requirements
- **Design System:** Plus Jakarta Sans, Sovereign Purple (#a319c5), Cloud Mist (#f8f6f7).
- **Layout:** Horizontal 5-step progress stepper at the top (Style, Time, Details, Review, Payment).
- **Right Panel:** Persisting "Booking Summary" panel showing selected Style, Time, and Price.

## Step 2: Time Selection
- **Features:** 
    - Interactive calendar/date picker for selecting service date.
    - Time slots categorized by "Morning", "Afternoon", and "Evening".
    - Clear "Selected" state for specific hours (e.g., 9:00 AM, 1:00 PM).
- **Action:** "Confirm Time" button.

## Step 3: Client Details
- **Features:** 
    - Form fields: Full Name, Email, Phone Number.
    - Text area for "Special Requirements / Inspiration notes".
    - Design: Elegant input fields with ample white space.
- **Action:** "Review Appointment" button.

## Step 4: Review Appointment
- **Features:** 
    - High-end summary grid showing:
        - **Style Selection:** Image and name.
        - **Date & Time:** Selected slot.
        - **Client Info:** Name and contact details.
    - Sub-total, tax (simulated), and total estimated price.
- **Action:** "Proceed to Payment" button.

## Step 5: Secure Payment
- **Features:** 
    - Modern credit card input field (Card number, MM/YY, CVC, ZIP).
    - Express checkout options (Apple Pay / Google Pay) placeholders.
    - "Pay & Confirm Booking" button in high-contrastbrand color.
    - Security trust badges (SSL, Secure checkout).
